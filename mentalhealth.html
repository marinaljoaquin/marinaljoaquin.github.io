Client: Millennials-September-April 2021 My Role: My responsibilities included User research, UI, UX and designing a branding visual identity design for this app..

Overview

I designed an interactive app where the user can freely express themselves within the app while also engaging, and being inspired by motivational quotes and stories about people who have dealt with hard times.

Problem

Theres a problem with Mental Health in the Millennial Generation. Millennials are growing up with more mental health issues. They are being discriminated. Having to deal withFinancial strain, loneliness, the strive for perfectionism, and workplace burnout. These are some of the top contributing factors associated with rising rates of mental health disorders among this generation.

Goal

I wanted to build an interactive app to raise the awareness on the Mental Health stigma geared towards Millennials who are having an emotional hard time looking for careers after college.I want to change the stigma and I want people to feel more freely to talk about mental health and how theyâ€™re dealing with it.

Process

I gathered data by researching about the stigma and currently what millennials are going through. I also did a qualitative interview with a few millennials to understand their pain points and needs. I also ran a survey on social media which on mine are mainly millennials asking them about their mental health experience. This data showed me that there is a stigma happening and that I would like to see a change in it.




Sketching + Ideation

I started gathering key terms that related to ice cream, thinking about it outside the box. I started sketching out icons, typography an using the key terms I came up with to inspire the logo.



Who are we designing for?

In order to understand how to design a tool for our users, I had to collect data from the qualititative interviews I conducted from current millennials who are struggling during these current times.
